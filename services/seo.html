How We Get Things Done

Initial Consultation

Keyword research and mapping

We use top-notch tools like SEMRush, Ahrefs, Keyword Planner, and Keywordtool.io to pinpoint the most valuable search terms people use to find your products and services. Then we connect those keywords to relevant pages on your site so visitors land where they need to be.

Competition analysis

Next, we closely review what your competitors are doing online—the terms they rank for, types of content published, and backlinks earned. The goal is to find any holes or opportunities we can take advantage of.

Strategy building

With all the research complete, we then build a comprehensive SEO plan tailored to your business and goals. Here, we focus on must-have details like ranking keywords, technical fixes, content topics, and link-building strategies critical for SEO success, basically complementing the previous step.

Execution

The best part is we handle every aspect needed to rank you above your competitors—writing optimized content, improving site speed and navigation, and building backlinks. We track timelines and benchmarks so we see regular progress and also share them with you every month.

We maintain proper reports

Our commitment to transparency is reflected in detailed monthly reports. These reports showcase rankings, traffic, and leads generated, and provide actionable recommendations. Going all out with local SEO practices

We enhance your local online presence by optimizing listings, citations, reviews, and on-page elements. We build citations on major sites like Yelp, Yellow Pages, the Better Business Bureau, and many more. Your business stands out in local searches and makes connecting with nearby customers seeking your products/services easier.

Optimizing website for mobiles

Making your website fast and functional on mobile is crucial today. We ensure your website is quick and mobile-friendly.
