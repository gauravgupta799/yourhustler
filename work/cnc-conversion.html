Client Brief

CNC Conversion Plus, founded in 2017, specializes in providing innovative CNC conversion kits and turn-key machines for benchtop mills and lathes.

Based in Houston, Texas, CNC Conversion Plus was born out of Michael R.'s passion and expertise in machining, with a mission to revolutionize small machine shop operations.

The Challenge

When we first engaged with Michael, his company's online presence was far from optimal. He had previously partnered with an agency that heavily relied on outdated tactics, yielding minuscule returns. At that juncture, his website was generating a mere trickle of traffic – fewer than 100 visits per month – and a paltry 5 leads, resulting in dismal e-commerce sales figures.

The website itself was plagued by a myriad of issues: a haphazard structure, convoluted navigation, and poorly optimized content that failed to resonate with its target audience. Moreover, a substantial portion of the product pages suffered from duplicated descriptions, further compounding the problems.

Solution

Our engagement commenced with a comprehensive website overhaul, as the existing platform was plagued by fundamental issues that impeded its ability to attract and convert visitors effectively. We commenced by building a new website from the ground up, meticulously architecting its structure and navigation to ensure an intuitive and seamless user experience.

Simultaneously, we devoted substantial efforts to revamping the product pages, which formed the crux of the website's e-commerce functionality. Our team crafted compelling, keyword-optimized descriptions that accurately conveyed each product's unique value proposition while adhering to best practices to eliminate duplicate content.

To improve search rankings, we optimized those pages for relevant keywords and built quality backlinks to them from other reputable sites.

We also fixed technical SEO issues like slow load times, mobile compatibility problems, and indexing errors that were hurting their search performance.

In addition to the product pages, we produced blog content on a consistent schedule to cover topics related to their industry. This helped establish their expertise and authority. Throughout the project, we tracked key metrics and adjusted our approach based on what was working and what wasn't. The goal was to align our SEO and content efforts with their overall business goals for sustainable growth and ROI.

We optimized their Google Business Profile, securing over 500 citations to enhance their presence in the Google Map Pack for more local leads in sauna and steam room installations.
