Client Brief

Florida Commercial Building Inspectors (FCBI) offers a diverse range of services in the commercial building inspection industry.

Known for their integrity, craftsmanship, and strong client relationships, they wanted a website that reflects their industry experience as well as early-bird integration of advanced tools and techniques.

We tailored their website using icons and colors that demonstrate clarity and confidence, something FCBI stands for. The final website is a detailed yet easy guide to FCBI's specialized services in commercial due diligence.
